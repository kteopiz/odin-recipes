Easy Microwave Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

This easy mug cake is easy to make with basic ingredients and comes together quickly in the microwave. What is not to love?

It couldn’t be easier to make a homemade mug cake: Combine the dry ingredients in a microwave-safe mug, stir in the wet ingredients, then cook in the microwave until the cake is done in the middle. You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below with detailed instructions.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a mug, stir until fully combined
  2. Cook in the microwave until cake is done in the middle about 1 minute and 45 seconds
